What is your favourite scripture at this point in your life and tell us what it means to you:

The past few months I have been feasting on the book of James. There are so many nuggets of wisdom in this book. My favourite is from chapter 1, verses 2 to 3. It says: 'Consider it pure joy, whenever you face trials of many kinds because you know that the testing of your faith produces perseverence.' It encourages me that whenever my faith is being tested, I know that it is for a good reason and that God is increasing my faith muscles.

Do you have anything to say to anyone that will be visiting this page?

Don't despise the season that you are in. Rejoice in the season that God has put you in. Remember that the testing of your faith produces steadfastness!
